My Blog

My Internship at BetterPitch: Turning Data into Design

March 1, 2025

As a Graphic Design Intern at BetterPitch in 2024, I had the chance to work on real-world business presentation projects. My main role was to turn complex business data into clean, engaging, and informative infographics. I used Adobe tools like InDesign and Photoshop to design assets that matched the company’s brand and communication goals.

This internship taught me how to simplify ideas visually and collaborate within a creative team. I also learned how to align design with storytelling — ensuring each visual not only looked good but clearly conveyed the message. It strengthened my belief that design isn't just about decoration — it's about communication.

Working in a real design environment helped sharpen my professional mindset and confirmed that graphic design is more than a skill — it’s a powerful tool for impact.

University Activities That Shaped My Creative Thinking

February 10, 2025

My time at Khon Kaen University has been full of meaningful experiences beyond the classroom. As the team head of Engineering KKU GEAR58 in 2022, I was the coordinator between the student union and engineering students — helping organize and lead creative university events.

I also participated in the KKU Photo Club, where I contributed to university-wide photography projects. This gave me the chance to express ideas visually, work with others, and build an eye for composition, lighting, and storytelling.

These activities pushed me to explore visual design in different contexts — from leadership and communication to art and emotion — all of which influence how I design today.
